Ring gauges ensure that external threads meet specified standards, while plug gauges validate the accuracy of internal threads. Other types may include custom gauges and specialized tools for specific applications. These gauges are essential in manufacturing, quality control, and ensuring interoperability in threaded parts across industries.</p></p>
