<p><p>The latex polymer market is divided into natural and synthetic latex polymers. Natural latex polymer is derived from rubber trees, offering biodegradability and eco-friendliness, commonly used in gloves, mattresses, and medical products. Synthetic latex polymer is chemically produced, providing enhanced durability, chemical resistance, and customization, widely used in paints, adhesives, and coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries, with natural latex favored for sustainability and synthetic latex for performance and versatility, driving demand across global markets.</p></p>
