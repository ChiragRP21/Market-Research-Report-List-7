<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Private LTE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDD</li><li>TDD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The private LTE market is primarily categorized into Frequency Division Duplex (FDD) and Time Division Duplex (TDD). FDD utilizes separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink communications, ensuring consistent performance and lower latency, making it suitable for applications requiring high data throughput. In contrast, TDD shares the same frequency for both uplink and downlink but allocates time slots for each direction, allowing flexible bandwidth usage and efficient resource management, ideal for variable traffic loads in dynamic environments.</p></p>
