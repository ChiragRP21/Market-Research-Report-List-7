<p><p>The Quantitative Immunoassay market includes various types, notably Electrochemiluminescence Immunoassays (ECLIA) and Bead-based Immunoassays. ECLIA utilizes electrochemical signals generated during chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity and specificity for analyte detection. Bead-based Immunoassays employ microspheres coated with specific antibodies, enabling multiplexing capabilities for simultaneous detection of multiple analytes. Both methods enhance diagnostic efficiency and accuracy, catering to diverse applications in clinical diagnostics, research, and pharmaceutical development. Their innovative technologies drive growth in the immunoassay market.</p></p>
