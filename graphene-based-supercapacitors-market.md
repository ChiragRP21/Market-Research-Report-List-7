<p><strong>The Graphene Based Supercapacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Double Layer Capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitor</li><li>Asymmetric Supercapacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphene-based supercapacitors are classified into three main types: Electrical Double Layer Capacitors (EDLCs), which store energy through the electrostatic separation of charges and offer high power density; Pseudocapacitors, which utilize fast redox reactions to enhance capacitance and energy density; and Asymmetric Supercapacitors, which combine features of both EDLCs and pseudocapacitors for improved energy storage and versatility. Each type leverages graphene's unique properties to enhance performance, making them suitable for various high-efficiency energy applications.</p></p>
