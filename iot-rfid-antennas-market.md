<p><strong>The IoT RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li><li>Semi-Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID antennas market is categorized into three main types: passive, active, and semi-active RFID. Passive RFID relies on external readers for power, making it cost-effective for applications like inventory tracking. Active RFID has its own power source, enabling longer read ranges and real-time tracking, ideal for asset management. Semi-active RFID combines features of both, with a battery powering the tag while still requiring a reader to communicate, suitable for applications where extended range and battery life are needed.</p></p>
