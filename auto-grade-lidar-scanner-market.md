<p><p>The Auto Grade LiDAR Scanner market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to capture detailed 3D maps, offering high accuracy but often at the cost of size and weight. In contrast, Solid State LiDAR employs fixed sensors and electronic scanning, resulting in a more compact, lightweight design with lower manufacturing costs. Both technologies play crucial roles in automotive applications, enhancing navigation, environment mapping, and safety systems.</p></p>
