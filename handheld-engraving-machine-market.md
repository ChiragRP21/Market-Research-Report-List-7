<p><p>The handheld engraving machine market primarily features two main types: mechanical engraving and laser engraving. Mechanical engraving utilizes rotating tools or bits to physically etch designs onto surfaces, making it suitable for various materials like wood and metal. In contrast, laser engraving employs focused laser beams to achieve precise and intricate designs with minimal material distortion, ideal for both delicate and complex patterns. Each type caters to different user needs, preferences, and applications in crafting and industrial sectors.</p></p>
