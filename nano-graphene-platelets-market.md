<p><p>The nano-graphene platelets market comprises two primary types: single-layer graphene nanosheets and bilayer graphene nanosheets. Single-layer graphene nanosheets are composed of a single atomic layer of carbon atoms arranged in a two-dimensional honeycomb lattice, offering superior electrical, thermal, and mechanical properties. Bilayer graphene nanosheets, made of two stacked layers, exhibit slightly different characteristics, including enhanced stability and conductivity. These materials find applications in diverse sectors, including electronics, energy storage, composites, and biomedical fields, driven by their advanced properties.</p></p>
