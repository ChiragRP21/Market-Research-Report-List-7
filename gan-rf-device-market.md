<p><p>The GaN RF device market consists of three primary types based on substrate material. GaN-on-Si utilizes silicon substrates, offering cost advantages and high volume manufacturing capabilities, making it popular for consumer electronics. GaN-on-SiC employs silicon carbide substrates, providing superior thermal management and efficiency, ideal for high-frequency and high-power applications in telecommunications and aerospace. GaN-on-Diamond features diamond substrates for exceptional thermal conductivity, often used in specialized applications requiring extreme performance, though it is less common due to higher costs.</p></p>
