<p><p>The marker board market encompasses three main types: melamine, porcelain, and glass marker boards. Melamine boards are cost-effective and suitable for light use, featuring a resin-coated surface for easy writing. Porcelain boards offer durability and resistance to stains and scratches, ideal for high-usage environments. Glass marker boards provide a sleek, modern look with a non-porous surface that doesn’t ghost, allowing for smooth writing and easy cleaning. Each type caters to different needs based on budget and usage intensity.</p></p>
