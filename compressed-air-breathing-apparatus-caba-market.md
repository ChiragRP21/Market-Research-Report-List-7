<p><strong>The Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus (CABA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit CABA</li><li>Closed-Circuit CABA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed Air Breathing Apparatus (CABA) are essential for providing breathable air in environments where oxygen is insufficient or hazardous. The market is categorized into two main types: Open-Circuit CABA, which expels exhaled air into the environment and supplies fresh air from a tank for each breath, making it suitable for short-duration tasks; and Closed-Circuit CABA, which recycles exhaled air by extracting carbon dioxide and adding oxygen, allowing for longer operation periods and better air quality in confined spaces.</p></p>
