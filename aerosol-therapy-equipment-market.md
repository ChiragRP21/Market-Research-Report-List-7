<p><strong>The Aerosol Therapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs)</li><li>Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol therapy equipment market encompasses various inhalation devices, primarily Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs). MDIs dispense a pre-measured dose of medication in aerosol form, requiring coordination between inhalation and activation. They are user-friendly and portable. DPIs, on the other hand, deliver medication as a dry powder, activated by the patient's inhalation. They do not require propellants and offer efficient drug delivery. Both types cater to patients with respiratory conditions, enhancing treatment adherence and control.</p></p>
