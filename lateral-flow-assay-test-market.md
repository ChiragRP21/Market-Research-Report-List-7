<p><p>Lateral Flow Assay Tests are diagnostic tools used to detect specific substances in samples. The market includes Sandwich Assays, which capture target analytes between two antibodies, allowing for sensitive detection. These are commonly used for identifying proteins or pathogens. Competitive Assays, on the other hand, involve a competition between the target analyte and a labeled variant for binding sites, typically offering rapid results for smaller molecules. Both types serve various applications, including clinical diagnostics, environmental testing, and food safety.</p></p>
