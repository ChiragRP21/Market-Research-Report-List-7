<p><p>Liquid mass flow meters are essential for measuring the mass flow rate of fluids in various applications. In closed pipe systems, these meters measure flow within enclosed pipes, ensuring accurate readings without environmental exposure. Open channel systems, on the other hand, gauge flow in open waterways, such as rivers or channels, using weirs or flumes. Both types serve vital roles across industries, including water management, chemical processing, and food production, providing crucial data for efficiency and compliance.</p></p>
