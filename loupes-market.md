<p><p>Loupes are specialized magnifying devices used in various fields like dentistry, surgery, and jewelry making. The Through The Lens (TTL) loupe offers a fixed focus for hands-free use, while Flip Up loupes provide versatility with the ability to pivot out of sight. Galilean loupes have a simpler optical design, delivering lightweight and compact magnification. Prismatic loupes utilize prisms for enhanced optical performance and wider viewing angles. Each type caters to specific user needs, enhancing precision and efficiency in tasks.</p></p>
