<p><ul><li>Two-stage</li><li>Three-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-submarine missile market can be categorized into two main types: two-stage and three-stage systems. Two-stage missiles typically employ a solid-fuel booster for initial launch and a second stage for terminal guidance, offering simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, three-stage missiles incorporate an additional stage, enhancing range and accuracy by optimizing propulsion and guidance capabilities. This differentiation allows defense forces to choose systems based on operational requirements, cost considerations, and the complexity of naval warfare scenarios.</p></p>
