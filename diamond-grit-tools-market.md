<p><p>Diamond grit tools are categorized into several types based on their bonding methods. Metal-bonded tools feature diamond grains held in a metal matrix, offering durability for grinding and cutting hard materials. Plated tools have diamonds electroplated onto a substrate, providing a cost-effective solution for finer applications. Ceramic-bonded tools utilize ceramic materials to bond diamonds, delivering high precision and thermal stability. Other categories may include hybrid or resin-bonded tools, each catering to specific industrial and manufacturing needs.</p></p>
