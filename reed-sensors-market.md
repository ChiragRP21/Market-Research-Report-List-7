<p><p>Reed sensors are magnetic switches that come in various forms, categorized primarily into Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A sensors are normally open and close in the presence of a magnetic field, making them ideal for security applications. Form B sensors are normally closed and open with a magnetic field, suitable for sensing applications where interruption is required. Form C sensors have both normally open and normally closed contacts, allowing for more versatile use. Other types include specialty sensors tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
