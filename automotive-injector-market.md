<p><strong>The Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-point injection</li><li>Continuous injection</li><li>Sequential injection</li><li>Direct injection</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Automotive Injector Market** includes various fuel injection systems. **Single-point injection** uses one injector in the throttle body, delivering fuel to all cylinders. **Continuous injection** supplies fuel constantly, often seen in older systems. **Sequential injection** delivers fuel to each cylinder at precise times, improving efficiency. **Direct injection** sprays fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing performance and fuel economy. **Others** include advanced or hybrid systems combining multiple technologies. Each type caters to specific engine requirements, balancing power, efficiency, and emissions control.</p></p>
