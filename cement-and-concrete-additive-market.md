<p><p>The cement and concrete additive market comprises three main types: chemical additives, mineral additives, and fiber additives. Chemical additives enhance the properties of cement and concrete, improving workability, durability, and setting times. Mineral additives, such as fly ash and slag, provide supplementary cementitious material, enhancing strength and sustainability. Fiber additives, including steel, glass, and synthetic fibers, strengthen concrete by reducing cracking and improving toughness. Together, these additives optimize construction performance, extending the lifespan and efficiency of concrete structures.</p></p>
