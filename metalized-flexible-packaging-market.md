<p><p>The metalized flexible packaging market includes various structure types tailored for specific applications. Mono extruded structures consist of a single layer, providing basic barrier properties. Insulated structures offer thermal protection, ideal for temperature-sensitive products. Laminated structures combine multiple materials for enhanced strength and barrier, making them suitable for food and pharmaceuticals. Co-extruded structures involve multiple layers extruded together, allowing for customized properties like moisture resistance and mechanical strength, catering to diverse market needs across industries.</p></p>
