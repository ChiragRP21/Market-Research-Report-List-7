<p><p>The mechanical air filter market consists of several types: Pre-filters, Sub-HEPA filters, and Secondary filters. Secondary filters complement primary systems by trapping remaining particulates, enhancing overall air quality. Together, these filters improve indoor air cleanliness, catering to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, thereby driving growth in the air filtration sector.</p></p>
