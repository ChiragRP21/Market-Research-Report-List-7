<p><p>The automotive catalyst market is primarily segmented into two main types: two-way catalysts and three-way catalysts. Two-way catalysts are designed to reduce carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons emissions, primarily used in diesel engines. In contrast, three-way catalysts perform multiple functions, effectively reducing carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides emissions, making them essential for gasoline engines. These catalysts play a crucial role in meeting stringent emissions regulations and improving air quality, driving their demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
