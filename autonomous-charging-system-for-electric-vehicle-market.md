<p><p>The Autonomous Charging System for Electric Vehicles (EVs) includes two market types: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and Fixed systems. AMRs are mobile units that navigate to EVs, offering flexible, on-demand charging without fixed infrastructure. They are ideal for dynamic environments like parking lots. Fixed systems are stationary charging stations installed at specific locations, providing consistent, high-power charging. Both aim to enhance EV convenience, with AMRs offering mobility and adaptability, while Fixed systems ensure reliability and efficiency in designated areas.</p></p>
