<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Car Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Electric Power Steering (EPS) market includes three main types: Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), and Rack-EPS (R-EPS). C-EPS integrates the motor on the steering column, offering cost efficiency and compact design, ideal for smaller vehicles. P-EPS places the motor on the pinion, balancing performance and cost, suitable for mid-sized cars. R-EPS positions the motor on the steering rack, providing superior power and precision, often used in larger or performance-oriented vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, balancing cost, space, and performance.</p></p>
