<p><strong>The Print Quality Inspection & Verification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Type</li><li>Off-line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Print Quality Inspection & Verification Systems are categorized into two primary types: in-line and off-line. In-line systems operate within the production process, continuously monitoring print quality in real-time, ensuring immediate detection of defects and enabling quick adjustments. Off-line systems, on the other hand, involve inspecting printed materials after production, allowing for thorough analysis and detailed verification of quality but lacking immediate intervention capabilities. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining print integrity and meeting quality standards in various industries.</p></p>
