<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct-driven Wind Turbine Generator Market can be classified into Type I and Type II. Type I systems feature a permanent magnet generator (PMG) directly connected to the rotor, offering high efficiency and fewer mechanical components. In contrast, Type II systems use a doubly-fed induction generator (DFIG) that allows for variable speed operation and provides better grid compliance. Both types cater to different operational needs and efficiency metrics, influencing their application in various wind farm projects.</p></p>
