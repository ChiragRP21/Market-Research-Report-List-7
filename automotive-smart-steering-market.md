<p><p>The Automotive Smart Steering Market is mainly divided into Electric Power Steering System (EPS) and Steering-By-Wire (SBW). EPS uses electric motors to assist steering, enhancing fuel efficiency and providing a more responsive driving experience. In contrast, SBW eliminates mechanical linkages between the steering wheel and wheels, relying on electronic signals for operation. This technology increases design flexibility and integration with advanced driver-assistance systems, paving the way for more innovative vehicle control solutions and improved safety features.</p></p>
