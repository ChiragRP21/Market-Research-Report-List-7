<p><p>Fiberglass tubing with silicon resin coating offers high thermal stability, excellent electrical insulation, and resistance to moisture, chemicals, and UV exposure, making it ideal for high-temperature and harsh environments. Polyurethane-coated fiberglass tubing provides superior flexibility, abrasion resistance, and durability, suitable for applications requiring mechanical strength and protection against wear and tear. Both types cater to industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction, with silicon resin excelling in extreme conditions and polyurethane offering robust performance in dynamic, high-stress environments.</p></p>
