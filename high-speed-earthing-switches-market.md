<p><ul><li>Independent Type</li><li>Combined Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed earthing switch market is categorized into two primary types: Independent Type and Combined Type. </p><p>Independent Type switches are standalone devices designed to rapidly ground electrical equipment, ensuring safety without integration into circuit breakers. </p><p>Combined Type switches, on the other hand, are integrated with circuit breakers, providing both switching and earthing functions in a single unit. This integration enhances operational efficiency and space utilization, making Combined Type switches increasingly popular in modern electrical systems.</p></p>
