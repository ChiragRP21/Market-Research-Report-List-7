<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrophoresis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrophoresis devices market comprises two main types: gel electrophoresis systems and capillary electrophoresis systems. Gel electrophoresis systems utilize a gel medium to separate biomolecules based on size and charge, commonly used in DNA, RNA, and protein analysis. In contrast, capillary electrophoresis systems employ narrow capillaries for rapid separation, offering higher resolution and efficiency, often favored for complex mixtures. Both types are essential in laboratories for applications in molecular biology, genetics, and biopharmaceuticals, driving advancements in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
