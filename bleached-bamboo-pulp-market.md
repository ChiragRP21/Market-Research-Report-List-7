<p><strong>The Bleached Bamboo Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Caustic Soda Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bleached bamboo pulp market comprises two primary types based on production processes: the sulfate process and the caustic soda process. The sulfate process, also known as the kraft process, utilizes sodium sulfate to chemically break down bamboo fibers, yielding strong, durable pulp suitable for various applications. Conversely, the caustic soda process employs sodium hydroxide to dissolve lignin, resulting in softer pulp with a higher brightness. Both methods cater to increasing demand for sustainable, eco-friendly pulp in the paper and packaging industries.</p></p>
