<p><p>The On Board Driver Monitoring System (DMS) market is divided into two main types: Active DMS and Passive DMS. Active DMS uses technologies such as cameras and sensors to continuously assess the driver’s attentiveness and behavior, issuing alerts if signs of distraction or fatigue are detected. In contrast, Passive DMS focuses on collecting data on the driver's condition without real-time intervention, primarily for analysis and reporting. Both systems aim to enhance road safety and improve overall driving performance.</p></p>
