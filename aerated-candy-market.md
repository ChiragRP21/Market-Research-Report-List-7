<p><p>The aerated candy market is categorized into three main types based on their air content: high aerated, medium aerated, and low aerated candy. High aerated candy features a significant amount of air, resulting in a light, fluffy texture and a pronounced mouthfeel. Medium aerated candy strikes a balance, offering satisfactory aeration for a softer bite. Low aerated candy has minimal air incorporation, leading to denser confections with a rich, chewy consistency. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences for texture and taste.</p></p>
