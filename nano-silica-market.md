<p><strong>The Nano-silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-type</li><li>S-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano-silica market is divided into two main types: P-type and S-type. P-type nano-silica, produced from pyrogenic processes, features high purity and specific surface area, making it ideal for applications in rubber, coatings, and adhesives. In contrast, S-type nano-silica, derived from sol-gel processes, offers diverse functionalities and is often used in electronics, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with varying properties influencing their applications and market demand in various sectors.</p></p>
