<p><p>The activated carbon market from agricultural waste is primarily categorized into two types: powder and particles. Powdered activated carbon (PAC) features fine granules, ideal for applications requiring rapid adsorption, such as water purification and air filtration. Particle activated carbon (PAC) consists of larger granules, offering slower adsorption but enhanced mechanical strength, making it suitable for gas treatment and solvent recovery. Both forms leverage agricultural byproducts, promoting sustainability while meeting diverse industrial needs for effective adsorption solutions.</p></p>
