<p><strong>The Laboratory Mortar Grinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Mill</li><li>Disc Refiner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory mortar grinder market comprises various types of equipment for sample preparation and material processing, primarily including ball mills and disc refiners. Ball mills use spherical grinding media to achieve fine particle size through impact and attrition, making them suitable for a wide range of materials. In contrast, disc refiners utilize rotating discs to effectively shear and refine materials, allowing for greater control over particle size and consistency. Both technologies are crucial in research and development across various industries.</p></p>
