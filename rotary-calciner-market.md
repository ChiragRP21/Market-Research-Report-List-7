<p><strong>The Rotary Calciner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Rotary Calciner</li><li>Indirect Rotary Calciner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Calciner market is primarily divided into two types: Ordinary Rotary Calciners and Indirect Rotary Calciners. Ordinary Rotary Calciners utilize direct heat application, exposing materials to combustion gases, making them suitable for various mineral and chemical applications. In contrast, Indirect Rotary Calciners employ an enclosed shell to heat materials without direct exposure to combustion gases, providing greater control over the process and reducing contamination risks. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as cement, minerals, and chemical processing.</p></p>
