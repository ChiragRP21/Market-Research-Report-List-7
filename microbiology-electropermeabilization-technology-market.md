The market is segmented into three main types: Exponential Decay Pulse, which applies a decreasing intensity over time; Square Wave Pulse, characterized by constant amplitude pulses that switch on and off sharply; and Time Constant Pulse, which uses a specific time interval to optimize cell permeability. Each type offers distinct advantages based on application needs and efficiency.</p></p>
