<p><p>The shaft encoder market is primarily divided into incremental and absolute types, along with other specialized variants. Incremental encoders generate pulses as the shaft rotates, providing relative position data, which is useful for speed and direction detection. In contrast, absolute encoders offer a unique position value for each shaft angle, allowing for accurate location tracking even after power loss. Other types may include specialty encoders for specific applications, such as multiturn or wireless encoders, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
