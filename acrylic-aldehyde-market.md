In contrast, the glycerol dehydration method utilizes glycerol, a renewable resource, providing a more sustainable alternative. Both methods cater to the increasing demand for acrylic aldehyde in industries like plastics, paints, and adhesives, reflecting a shift towards environmentally friendly production practices while maintaining economic viability.</p></p>
