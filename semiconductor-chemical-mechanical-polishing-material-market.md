<p><p>The Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material Market encompasses two primary types: CMP slurry and CMP pads. CMP slurry is a fluid that contains abrasive particles and chemical additives, utilized to achieve surface planarization of semiconductor wafers during manufacturing. CMP pads, on the other hand, are the surfaces against which wafers are polished; they provide mechanical support and help control the polishing process. Together, these materials are essential for enhancing the performance and reliability of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
