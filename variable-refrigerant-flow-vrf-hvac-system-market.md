<p><ul><li>2 Pipe Systems</li><li>3 Pipe Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) HVAC systems are classified into two main types: 2-pipe systems and 3-pipe systems. </p><p>2-pipe systems circulate refrigerant for both heating and cooling, requiring a switch between modes, making them suitable for applications where simultaneous heating and cooling are not required. </p><p>In contrast, 3-pipe systems allow for simultaneous heating and cooling across different zones, utilizing an additional pipe for heat recovery. This makes 3-pipe systems more versatile, ideal for larger buildings with varying needs.</p></p>
