<p><ul><li>α Crystal</li><li>β Crystal</li><li>γ Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium oxide nanoparticles are categorized into three crystal types: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form, known as corundum, is the most stable and widely used for applications requiring durability and high thermal stability. β crystals are less common and typically exhibit different morphological and optical properties, while γ crystals are amorphous and used primarily in specific catalytic and adsorption applications. Each type offers distinct characteristics, influencing their utilization across industries such as electronics, healthcare, and materials science.</p></p>
