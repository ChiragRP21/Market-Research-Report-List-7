Iron-Based superalloys are known for their cost-effectiveness and are utilized in less demanding conditions, often in automotive and industrial applications. Cobalt-Based superalloys, recognized for their strength and stability in extreme environments, are typically used in high-performance sectors like aerospace and medical devices.</p></p>
