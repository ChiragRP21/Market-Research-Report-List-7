<p><p>The dissolvable sutures market comprises two main types: natural and synthetic dissolvable sutures. Natural dissolvable sutures are derived from biological materials such as gut or collagen, offering biocompatibility and promoting natural healing. In contrast, synthetic dissolvable sutures are engineered from polymers, providing customized absorption rates and strength. Both types are widely used in surgical procedures, with preferences varying based on factors like the type of surgery, healing time, and patient-specific considerations, driving their market growth.</p></p>
