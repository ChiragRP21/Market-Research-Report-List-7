<p><p>The unitary air conditioner market consists primarily of two types: split type and packaged type. Split type air conditioners feature two separate units—one indoors for cooling and the other outdoors for heat exchange—providing flexible installation and quieter operation. In contrast, packaged type units combine all components into a single outdoor unit, making them ideal for commercial spaces with space constraints. Both types cater to diverse cooling needs, balancing efficiency, design, and installation preferences.</p></p>
