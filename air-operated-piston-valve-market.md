<p><p>The air-operated piston valve market is segmented into 2-way, 3-way, 4-way, and other types. A 2-way valve controls flow in a single direction or allows diversion, typically used for on/off applications. A 3-way valve can direct flow between two outlets, making it ideal for mixing or diverting processes. A 4-way valve manages the flow to and from multiple positions, commonly found in actuating systems. Other valves may include specialized configurations catering to unique industrial needs.</p></p>
