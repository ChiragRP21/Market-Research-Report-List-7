<p><ul><li>Capacitive Touch</li><li>Infrared Touch</li><li>Resistive Touch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Interactive Smart Blackboard market encompasses three key types: Capacitive Touch, Infrared Touch, and Resistive Touch. Capacitive Touch uses electric fields to detect user interaction, offering high sensitivity and clarity, making it ideal for multitouch applications. Infrared Touch employs an array of infrared light beams to sense touch, providing excellent durability and performance in various lighting conditions. Resistive Touch relies on pressure applied to two flexible layers, making it cost-effective and suitable for environments where durability is crucial.</p></p>
