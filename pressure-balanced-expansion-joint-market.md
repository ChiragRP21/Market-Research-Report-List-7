<p><p>The Pressure Balanced Expansion Joint market is categorized primarily into Inline and Elbow Type. Inline expansion joints are designed for straight piping runs, accommodating thermal expansion and contraction while maintaining system pressure. They typically offer simpler installation and maintenance. Elbow Type expansion joints, on the other hand, are used in piping systems that require directional changes. They absorb thermal movement in multiple directions and are crucial in preventing stress on connected equipment, effectively enhancing system longevity and reliability.</p></p>
