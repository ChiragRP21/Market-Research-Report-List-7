<p><p>The White Masonry Cement market is primarily categorized into Type S and Type N. Type S is a structural cement designed for high-strength applications, often used in load-bearing structures and exterior masonry work. It provides enhanced durability and resistance to moisture. Type N, on the other hand, is a general-purpose cement suitable for most masonry applications, offering a balance of strength and workability, ideal for non-load-bearing walls and interiors. Both types cater to varying construction needs and preferences.</p></p>
