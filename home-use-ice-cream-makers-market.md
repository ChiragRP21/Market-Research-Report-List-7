<p><p>The home use ice cream makers market features two primary types: soft ice cream machines and hard ice cream machines. Soft ice cream machines produce a creamy, light texture ideal for frozen treats like soft-serve ice cream and frozen yogurt. These machines typically have a built-in compressor for easy dispensing. In contrast, hard ice cream machines create denser, traditional ice cream with a firmer consistency, often requiring pre-freezing of bowls or using built-in freezing mechanisms for optimal results.</p></p>
