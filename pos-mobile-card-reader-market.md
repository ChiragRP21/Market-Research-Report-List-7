<p><p>The PoS mobile card reader market is primarily divided into two types: EMV technology and non-EMV technology. EMV (Europay, MasterCard, and Visa) technology enhances security through chip-enabled cards, requiring customers to input a PIN or signature for transactions. This reduces fraud and is widely adopted by merchants. Non-EMV technology typically involves magnetic stripe cards that are less secure, allowing for simpler swipe transactions but posing higher fraud risks. Consequently, the EMV segment is experiencing greater growth due to increasing security demands from consumers and businesses.</p></p>
