<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) market is primarily divided into two types based on production processes: Phosgene Type and Non-Phosgene Type. The Phosgene Type involves the use of phosgene in its polymerization process, commonly producing high-quality, transparent polycarbonate with superior optical properties. In contrast, the Non-Phosgene Type employs alternative methods, such as melt polymerization, avoiding toxic chemicals, and thereby offering a more environmentally friendly approach. Both types cater to various applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
