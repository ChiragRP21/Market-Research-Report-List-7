<p><strong>The Multifocal Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refractive Type</li><li>Diffraction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifocal intraocular lens (IOL) market is primarily divided into two types: refractive type and diffraction type. Refractive multifocal IOLs utilize varied lens curvatures to focus light at multiple distances, providing enhanced vision clarity. In contrast, diffraction type IOLs employ diffraction patterns to distribute light and create simultaneous images at different focal points. Both types aim to reduce dependence on glasses after cataract surgery, but they offer distinct optical designs and visual experiences tailored to patient needs.</p></p>
